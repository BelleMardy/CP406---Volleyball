Welcome to the world of Volleyball.GAME OVERVIEW

  • Team sport - one team against another
  • Played on a court of sand separated only by a net
  • Orignated in the USA but now a global recognised game
  • 800 millions players in any one week gracing the courts
  • High action sport that requires great skill
  • Aim of the game: force your opponents, through tactical manoeuvres, to miss returning the ball back over the net
