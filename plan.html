    CLIENT MISSION STATEMENT

    Promote awareness and attendance of Slammers Beach Volleyball, resulting in increased revenue by minimum 50%, while also promotoing healthy alternative for fitness among 15 to 30 year olds. Advertise venue hire opportunities and existing social activities.

    Success Evaluation

    To determining success the following evaluation methods will be employed:

    • Utilise free assessment tools
    • Google Analytics
    • Rollyo
    • Gain feedback from client and members
    • Have new members fill out a survey when they join, for example add to the membership paperwork
    • How did you hear about Slammers Beach Volleyball?
    • Would you like anything added to the website?
    • Was the website easy to use?

      • On membership, they have members only access where there is a forum that they can add comments to which would allow for monitoring. Have a comments option available through electronic mediums.

    • Establish what information that could be added to website from that feedback, and adjust accordingly.
    • Monitor that clients needs are meet through
    • Statistical collation
    • Increased membership (increase revenue)
    • Point of sale data collection, food, beverages (including type)
    • Actual attendance
    • Hire of venue data (increase/decrease)
    • Feedback from client about how they like the website
    • Have the website tested in an attempt to “break” and respond to “bug’s” as appropriate.
    • Utilise the "Call To Action" submit function from the website to monitor viability and usability of website in current format.
    • Obtain personal details eg, age, gender, and if they have friends or family members interested in joining
